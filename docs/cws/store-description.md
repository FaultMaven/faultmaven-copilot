## Detailed Description
FaultMaven Copilot brings AI-powered incident investigation and troubleshooting right into your browser's side panel. Stop context-switching between your observability dashboards, runbooks, and chat tools during critical incidents.

**Key Features:**
*   **Context-Aware Troubleshooting:** AI that understands your active cases and live telemetry.
*   **Knowledge Base Integration:** Seamlessly correlates live issues with your existing knowledge base content and runbooks.
*   **Capture Page Context:** Easily capture error traces, logs, and dashboard context with a single click.
*   **Secure & Private:** Works with your self-hosted or SaaS backend. We only capture what you explicitly share.

Whether you're an on-call engineer, SRE, or DevOps practitioner, FaultMaven Copilot helps you resolve incidents faster by putting the right context at your fingertips.

## Privacy Highlights
*   Does not read your browsing history.
*   Does not capture screen data without explicit user action.
*   Requires runtime host permissions only for the specific backend URL you configure.
